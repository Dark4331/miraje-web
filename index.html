What Miraje Does

Your AI, Your Rules

Miraje isn’t another cloud AI wrapper. It’s a local-first assistant designed to respect your autonomy, your data, and your workflow.

01

Privacy First

Every conversation, every file, every prompt stays on your machine. No telemetry, no cloud uploads, no middlemen. Miraje was built from day one with a zero-knowledge architecture — your data is never visible to anyone but you.

02

Email Reading

Miraje can connect to your local email client and intelligently read, summarize, and triage your inbox. Draft replies, flag priorities, and surface what matters — all without sending a single byte of your mail to an external server.

03

Skill & Improvement

Miraje learns from your feedback over time — not by uploading data, but by adapting locally. It picks up on your preferences, refines its responses, and grows alongside you without ever compromising your privacy.

04

Assistance, Not Replacement

Miraje is here to amplify your capabilities — not replace them. It drafts, suggests, and organizes, but the final call is always yours. Think of it as a trusted copilot that respects your judgment and never oversteps.
